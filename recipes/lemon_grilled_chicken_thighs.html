Lemon Grilled Chicken Thighs Recipe

This is a recipe that's great for beginners! It is an easy lemony-garlic dish that's great for keeping your protein intake up!

Ingredients

  • 1 lemon, zested and juiced
  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon grated garlic
  • 3 sprigs fresh parsley
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 8 chicken thighs
