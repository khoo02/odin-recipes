Grilled Asparagus

Here is another recipe that's perfect for beginners! Grilled Asparagus is easy to make, and only requires a few ingredients to get started!

Ingredients

  • Asparagus: One pound of fresh, clean, trimmed asparagus.
  • Oil: Olive oil works best, and helps the seasoning stick to the asparagus.
  • Seasonings: Salt and pepper works just fine! Garlic powder can also be added.
